Infosys
IT Services · NSE: INFY
Watch
+
// Research Notes
One of India's premier IT exporters. Revenue heavily USD-denominated, making it a natural hedge for currency volatility. Strong client retention metrics and large deal wins in cloud transformation and AI services. Management has been focusing on margin recovery post-pandemic hiring surge.
// Long-Term Thesis
AI/cloud transformation wave is a multi-year tailwind. Enterprise IT spending doesn't disappear — it shifts. Infosys is positioned to capture AI services revenue. Valuation is not cheap but reasonable for the moat.
// Risks
  • US recession impact on IT budgets
  • Rupee appreciation headwind on revenue
  • AI commoditizing lower-value IT services
  • Attrition rate and wage inflation pressure

HDFC
HDFC Bank
Banking & Finance · NSE: HDFCBANK
Hold
+
// Research Notes
India's largest private sector bank. Post-merger with HDFC Ltd, the balance sheet is massive. Loan book quality is best-in-class — GNPA ratios consistently below peers. Retail banking penetration still has massive headroom in India.
// Long-Term Thesis
India's credit-to-GDP is still very low. As formalization increases, HDFC is the prime beneficiary. Digital banking moat is widening, not shrinking. Long-term compounder play.
// Risks
  • Post-merger integration complexity
  • Deposit growth lagging loan growth
  • RBI policy tightening cycle
